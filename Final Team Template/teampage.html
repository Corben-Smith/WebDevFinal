Background

23XI Racing is a NASCAR team co-owned by former NBA player Michael Jordan and NASCAR driver Denny Hamlin. The team was established to compete in the NASCAR Cup Series, which is the top level of professional stock car racing in the United States.

About the Team

Bubba Wallace is the primary driver for 23XI Racing. He is notable as one of the few African American drivers in NASCAR and has been an advocate for diversity and inclusion in the sport. 23XI Racing aligns with Toyota as its manufacturer. 23XI Racing's entry into NASCAR represented a shift in the landscape, showcasing that the sport could attract major figures from other sports. This development signaled the potential for growth and expansion in NASCAR's appeal beyond its traditional fanbase. 23XI Racing has emphasized a commitment to promoting diversity, equality, and inclusion within the sport. The team aims to provide opportunities for underrepresented groups in NASCAR.
